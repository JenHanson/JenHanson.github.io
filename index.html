About Me

My name is Jennifer Hanson and I am a 3D artist and graphics designer. I’ve always had a passion for drawing and animation and quickly became hooked on 3D modeling in high school. I started seeing polygons in my dreams and my ambitions grew in college! After graduating, I enjoyed a freelance position and then a short in-house internship with Captured Dimensions in 2015. I am currently working as an alumni mentor and character modeler on an animated short with several UTD students. My goals involve becoming a valuable member of a 3D studio and completing a suit of 3D Mandalorian armor I have been working on. I have a great interest in all things creative and am excited for what I will create in 2016! View my past work , or contact me now if you're ready to get started!
